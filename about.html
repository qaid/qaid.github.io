About Me

I'm a designer and thinker cut from a different cloth. I've done creative and strategic work in 5 different industries over the past 15 years. Catch me in the DJ booth if I'm not on the basketball court.

My approach to design is rooted in curiosity and attention to detail. I believe that great design happens at the intersection of user needs, business goals, and technical possibilities. Every project is an opportunity to solve meaningful problems and create experiences that matter.
